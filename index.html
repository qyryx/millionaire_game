GAME RULES

Drag and drop the items into the right order. Click CHECK to review your order. If it's right, you continue to the next level, if not, you loose 1 health. If you loose all your 7 healths, the game is over. If you manage to order all 10 levels right, you win. Click Hint to see just one hint or click Solution to reveal the solution. By revealing solution you loose 1 health, so this feature is available if you have 2 healths at least.
